About Tom Lowes

After more than two decades as an osteopath, I've developed a unique perspective on back pain that bridges clinical expertise with real-world application.

Working with high-profile clients taught me that conventional approaches often fall short when bodies need to perform regardless of pain. These high-stakes environments forced me to develop more effective, evidence-based strategies that address not just the physical aspects of back pain, but the crucial movement confidence that's so often lost.

While my experience with celebrities provided invaluable insights, my passion lies in making these same transformative approaches accessible to everyone. I've observed identical patterns of movement hesitation in office workers that I've seen on film sets.

I've dedicated the last ten years to refining this method. The Back Coach incorporates everything I've learned working with elite performers, professional athletes, and thousands of everyday people who simply want to move freely again.
